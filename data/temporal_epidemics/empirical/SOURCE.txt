sp_hypertext_contacts.csv
=========================
Temporal face-to-face contact network of the ACM Hypertext 2009 conference
(113 attendees, 20 s resolution, ~59 h). Columns: source, target, time (seconds).

Source: Netzschleuder network repository, dataset `sp_hypertext`
        (https://networks.skewed.de/net/sp_hypertext), retrieved 2026-07-05.
Original data: SocioPatterns project (http://www.sociopatterns.org).
Reference: L. Isella, J. Stehle, A. Barrat, C. Cattuto, J.-F. Pinton,
           W. Van den Broeck, "What's in a crowd? Analysis of face-to-face
           behavioral networks", J. Theor. Biol. 271 (2011) 166-180.

Redistributed here for reproducibility of the epidemic study; please cite the reference above.
